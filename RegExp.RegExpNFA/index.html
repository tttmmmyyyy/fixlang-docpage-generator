    RegExp.RegExpNFA

    NFA (Nondeterministic Finite Automaton). This is internal module of RegExp.

    For details, see web pages below. - https://swtch.com/~rsc/regexp/regexp1.html - https://zenn.dev/canalun/articles/regexp_and_automaton
